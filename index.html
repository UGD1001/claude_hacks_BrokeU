Budgeting 101
Unlocked after Week 1 · 3 min read
A budget is simply a plan for your money. It tells every dollar where to go before the month begins — instead of wondering where it all went after it's already spent.

The core idea: Income − Expenses = What's left. If that number is negative, you're spending more than you earn. In the game, that directly slows your goal progress — exactly like real life.
Income  −  Fixed Expenses  −  Variable Expenses  =  Net Cash Flow
Fixed expenses stay the same every month: rent, subscriptions, loan payments. Variable expenses change: groceries, going out, transport. Most players overspend on variable costs early on — $14 here, $22 there — and miss their goal by minutes.
!
Game Tip
Activate High-Yield Savings the moment the game starts — it's risk-free and compounds your balance passively while you handle income decisions. It unlocks faster access to Index Funds at $3k.
